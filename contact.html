Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?
Simply fill out the contact form above, and our team will get in touch within 24 hours to discuss your needs and schedule a free trial class if interested.
What do I need for online classes?
You'll need a computer or tablet with stable internet, a webcam, and a microphone. We provide all learning materials and access to our online platform.
Can I enroll multiple family members?
Yes! We offer special family discounts for multiple students. Mention the number of learners in your message, and we'll provide personalized pricing options.
What if I'm a complete beginner?
No problem at all! We have beginner-friendly courses starting from the Arabic alphabet and gradually building your recitation and Tajweed skills.
