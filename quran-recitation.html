Quran Recitation

Learn proper Quran recitation with expert guidance for all levels

Course Overview

  • Level: All Levels
  • Duration: 6-12 months (depending on pace)
  • Frequency: 3-5 sessions per week
  • Prerequisites: Basic Arabic reading (or start with Noorani Qaida)
  • Language: English/Arabic

What You'll Learn

This comprehensive course covers complete Quran recitation from Juz 1-30 with proper Tajweed rules, deep Tafseer understanding, and essential Islamic practices. Perfect for those who want to master the art of Quran recitation.

Detailed Curriculum

  • Complete Tajweed rules and practical application
  • Makharij al-Huruf (articulation points of letters)
  • Sifaat al-Huruf (attributes of letters)
  • Rules of Noon Sakinah, Tanween, and Meem Sakinah
  • All types of Madd (elongation) rules
  • Comprehensive Tafseer of selected Surahs and Juz
  • Perfect Salah, essential Duas, and daily Islamic practices
  • Full Quran reading from Juz 1-30 with detailed correction
  • Rules of Waqf & Ibtida (stopping and starting)
  • Common recitation errors and how to avoid them
  • Introduction to different Qira'at (recitation styles)
  • Practical application in Salah and daily recitation
