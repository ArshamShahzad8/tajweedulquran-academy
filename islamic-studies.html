Islamic Studies

Essential Islamic knowledge including Fiqh, Aqeedah, Seerah, and Hadith alongside Quran learning

Course Overview

  • Level: Adults
  • Duration: 12-18 months
  • Frequency: 2-3 sessions per week
  • Prerequisites: Basic Quran knowledge
  • Language: English/Arabic

What You'll Learn

Comprehensive study of Islamic sciences including core beliefs, worship practices, Prophet's life, and Hadith. Perfect for deepening your understanding of Islam.

Detailed Curriculum

  • Core Islamic beliefs (Aqeedah)
  • Fiqh of worship (Salah, Zakat, Sawm, Hajj)
  • Life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) - Seerah
  • Selected Hadith studies and authentication
  • Islamic manners and ethics (Akhlaq)
  • Contemporary issues from Islamic perspective
  • Family life and relationships in Islam
  • Islamic history and civilization
  • Da'wah and calling to Islam
  • Understanding different Islamic schools
  • Women in Islam
  • Islamic finance and economics basics
