Quran Memorization (Hifz)

Structured Hifz program with revision plans for complete Quran memorization

Course Overview

  • Level: All Levels
  • Duration: 2-5 years (depending on dedication)
  • Frequency: Daily sessions (5-7 days/week)
  • Prerequisites: Basic Quran reading with Tajweed
  • Language: Arabic with English support

What You'll Learn

This comprehensive Hifz program guides you through memorizing the entire Quran with structured revision schedules, retention techniques, and one-on-one correction sessions.

Detailed Curriculum

  • Daily memorization targets with Tajweed accuracy focus
  • Structured revision schedules (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Advanced techniques for long-term retention
  • One-on-one listening and correction sessions
  • Memorization of selected Juz first, then complete Quran
  • Understanding meanings during memorization process
  • Preparation for Ijazah (authorization) certification
  • Motivational support and progress tracking
  • Revision of previously memorized portions
  • Final Quran completion and verification
  • Post-Hifz revision maintenance program
  • Teaching methodology for future Hafiz
