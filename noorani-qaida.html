Noorani Qaida

Perfect starting course for children and beginners to learn Arabic script and reading

Course Overview

  • Level: Beginner
  • Duration: 2-4 months
  • Frequency: 3-5 sessions per week
  • Prerequisites: None
  • Language: English/Arabic

What You'll Learn

This foundational course teaches Arabic letters, pronunciation, and basic reading skills. Designed for children and absolute beginners with fun, interactive lessons.

Detailed Curriculum

  • Arabic alphabet recognition (Huroof-e-Mufridaat)
  • Letter shapes and connecting forms
  • Short & long vowels (Harakat & Madd)
  • Sukun, Tanween, and Shaddah basics
  • Correct pronunciation of Quranic letters
  • Introduction to basic Tajweed rules
  • Reading simple words and short verses
  • Compound letters and word formation
  • Basic Islamic terms and Salah vocabulary
  • Fun exercises and interactive activities
  • Progress assessment and encouragement
  • Preparation for Quran reading courses
