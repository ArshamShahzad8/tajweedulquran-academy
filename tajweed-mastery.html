Tajweed Mastery

Master Tajweed rules and pronunciation for beautiful Quran recitation

Course Overview

  • Level: Intermediate
  • Duration: 4-6 months
  • Frequency: 3-4 sessions per week
  • Prerequisites: Basic Quran reading skills
  • Language: English/Arabic

What You'll Learn

This advanced course dives deep into complex Tajweed rules for accurate and beautiful Quran recitation. Learn from expert teachers with detailed explanations and practical application.

Detailed Curriculum

  • Detailed study of Makharij al-Huruf (articulation points)
  • Sifaat al-Huruf (attributes of letters) in depth
  • Advanced rules of Noon Sakinah and Tanween
  • Complex Meem Sakinah applications
  • All types of Madd: Tabi'i, Munfasil, Muttasil, Lazim
  • Ghunnah rules and nasalization techniques
  • Qalqalah (echoing) rules for emphatic letters
  • Tafkheem (heaviness) and Tarqeeq (lightness) rules
  • Waqf signs and proper stopping/starting techniques
  • Complex verse practice with detailed correction
  • Introduction to recitation melodies (Maqamat)
  • Advanced error identification and correction
