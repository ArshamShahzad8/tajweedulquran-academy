Quran Tafseer

In-depth study of Quran meanings, context, and lessons from authentic sources

Course Overview

  • Level: Advanced
  • Duration: 12-24 months
  • Frequency: 2-3 sessions per week
  • Prerequisites: Quran reading with Tajweed
  • Language: English/Arabic

What You'll Learn

Explore the deep meanings of the Quran through authentic Tafseer sources. Understand historical context, rulings, and practical lessons for modern life.

Detailed Curriculum

  • Tafseer of selected Juz and complete Surahs
  • Historical context (Asbab al-Nuzul)
  • Understanding Islamic rulings (Ahkam)
  • Lessons and morals for daily life
  • Comparison of classical Tafseer works
  • Discussion and reflection sessions
  • Study of major Tafseer scholars
  • Application of Quranic teachings
  • Scientific miracles in the Quran
  • Social and ethical guidance
  • Stories of Prophets and lessons
  • Contemporary issues from Islamic perspective
