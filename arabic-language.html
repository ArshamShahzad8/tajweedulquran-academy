Arabic Language

Understand Quranic Arabic deeply for better comprehension of the Holy Quran

Course Overview

  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Duration: 8-12 months
  • Frequency: 3 sessions per week
  • Prerequisites: Basic Arabic reading
  • Language: English/Arabic

What You'll Learn

Master Quranic Arabic grammar, vocabulary, and sentence structure to understand the meanings and context of the Holy Quran. Learn from experienced teachers with practical applications.

Detailed Curriculum

  • Basic Arabic grammar (Sarf - morphology)
  • Nahw (syntax) fundamentals
  • Common Quranic vocabulary and terminology
  • Sentence structure and verb conjugations
  • Reading and translating simple Quranic verses
  • Introduction to classical Arabic texts
  • Application in understanding Tafseer
  • Arabic writing and composition
  • Advanced grammar concepts (I'rab)
  • Balagha (rhetoric) basics
  • Translation of complete Surahs
  • Practical conversation in Modern Standard Arabic
